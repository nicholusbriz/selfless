# Student Registration Instructions

## 📋 Important Information for Students

### **Already Registered?**
If you have already created an account and selected your preferred cleaning day, follow these steps:

---

## 🔐 **Login to Your Account**

1. **Go to the Login Page**
   - Click "Login to System" on the main page
   - Enter your registered email address
   - Click "🚀 Access System"

2. **Submit Your Course Information**
   - After logging in, click "📚 Send Number of Credits"
   - Enter **ALL** course units you are currently taking
   - Add the number of credits for each course unit
   - Click "Register Courses & Credits" to save

---

## 📚 **Course Units to Include**

**Please enter ALL of your course units, for example:**
- Mathematics 101 - 3 credits
- Computer Science 201 - 4 credits  
- English Literature 102 - 3 credits
- Physics 101 - 4 credits
- Chemistry 201 - 3 credits

**Important:** Make sure to include every course unit you are enrolled in this semester.

---

## ✅ **What You Need to Complete**

- ✅ Account created and registered for cleaning day
- ✅ Login with your registered email
- ✅ Submit all course units with credit counts
- ✅ Complete course registration

---

## 🤝 **Need Help?**

If you have any questions or need assistance:
- Contact the Freedom City Tech Center administration
- Ask a staff member during your next visit
- Check with your course coordinator

---

**Thank you for participating in the Freedom City Tech Center cleaning program!** 🌟
